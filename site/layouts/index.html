Built for people who
live in their calendar

Every feature designed to reduce visual noise and increase focus.

Smart Event Merging

Overlapping events from multiple calendars automatically merge into a single block with a beautiful gradient showing all calendar colors. No duplicates. No clutter.

Focus Mode

Dim non-primary calendars to keep attention on what matters. Hovering reveals the full event.

Modern UI Polish

Rounded events, smooth shadows, frosted glass header, refined typography. Your calendar, elevated.

Next Event Highlight

A subtle glow marks your next upcoming event, so you always know what’s next at a glance.

Clean Layout

Auto-collapsing sidebar, hidden toolbar clutter, soft grid lines. Everything gets out of your way.

Dark Mode

Full dark mode support that follows your system preference. Or pick your theme manually.

Privacy is not a feature.
It’s a principle.

Everything is processed locally in your browser. CalBlend never sends your data anywhere — zero network requests, zero external servers. Your events are read, merged, and rendered entirely on your device. No analytics. No tracking. No accounts. Your calendar stays yours.
